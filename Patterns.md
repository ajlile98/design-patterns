Builder - ApartmentBuilder, QueryBuilder, EmailBuilder
Prototype - GameCharacter, DocumentTemplate, GraphNode

Facade - HomeTheaterFacade, BankingFacade, ComputerFacade

Proxy - ImageProxy, DatabaseProxy, SecurityProxy
